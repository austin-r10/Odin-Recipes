Honey Glazed Chicken

I found this chicken recipe online and decided to try it myself. I fell in love with it and had to share it with others. It hits the spot every time. Enjoy it!!

This is a quick and easy dish to make for you and the family. It will only take Prep Time: 10 minutes and Cook Time: of 10n minutes. This dish can Serve up to four people and each serving consist of 179 Calories Rand 13g of Protein.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
